Dataset: nigeria_singapore_hotel.csv

Sources:

Oluseyi, P.O., O.M. Babatunde, O.A. Batatunde (2016). "Assessment of Energy
Consumption and Carbon Footprint from the Hotel Sector Within Lagos, Nigeria,"
Energy and Buildings, Vol. 118, pp. 106-113.

Priyadarsini, R. W. Xuchao, L.S. Eang (2009). "A Study on Energy Performance of
Hotel Buildings in Singapore," Energy and Buildings, Vol. 41, pp. 1319-1324.

Description: Data on annual energy consumption, floor area, and number of employees
for 27 Lagos and 28 Singapore hotels.

Variables:
city    (1=Lagos, 2=Singapore)
hotel.ID   (within city)
year.EC    (annual energy consumption, MWh)
flr.area   (square meters)
employees
