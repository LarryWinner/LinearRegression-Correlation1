Dataset: bac_vhac.csv

Source: J.D. Caughlin (1983). "Correlation of Postmortem Blood and Vitreous Humor
Alcohol Concentration," Canadian Society of Forensic Science Journal, Vol. 16, #2, pp. 61-68

Description:
Blood and Vitreous Humor levels (mg%) in 43 cadavers. Author fit linear regression
with Y=BAC and X=VHAC

VHAC
