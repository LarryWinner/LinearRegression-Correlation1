Dataset: decanter_lead.csv

Source: E. Guadagnino, L. Gramiccioni, M. Denaro, M. Baldini (1998).
"Co-operative Study on the Release of Lead from Crystalware,"
Packaging Technology and Science, Vol. 11, pp.45-57.

Description: Experiment relating the amount of lead migrating into
brandy in unusded decanters (leadMig) as a function of the number of days
in the decanter (days). Each observation is average of 5 decanters.
