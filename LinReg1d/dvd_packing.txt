Dataset:  dvd_packing.csv

Source: H. Oktem (2012). "Modeling and Analysis of Process Parameters
for Evaluating Shrinkage Problems During Plastic Injection Molding of a
DVD-ROM Cover," Journal of Materials Engineering and Performance,
Vol. 21, #1, pp. 25-32.

Description: Experiment with 5 Factors and 27 observations relating
DVD cover shinkage (%) to 5 factors with the following levels:

Mold Temperature (C):  40, 60, 80
Melt Temperature (C):  230, 240, 250
Injection Pressure (Mpa):  80, 100, 120
Injection Time (sec): 1, 2, 3
Cooling Time (sec): 10, 15, 20

Variable Names:
runNum
moldTemp
meltTemp
injectPrs
injectTime
coolTime
shrinkPct
