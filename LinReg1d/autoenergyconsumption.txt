Dataset:  autoenergyconsumption.csv

Source: G. Golkarnarenji, M. Naebe, K. Badii, A.S. Milani, R.N. Jazar,
H. Khayyam (2018). "Support Vector Regression Modelling and Optimization
of Energy Consumption in Carbon Fiber Production Line," Computers and
Chemical Engineering, Vol. 109, pp. 276-288.

Description: Results of an experiment relating 3 factors to energy
consumption in cars.
Y = Energy Consumption, in MJ  (energyCon)
X1 = Temperature in celsius     (tempC)
Fiber space velicity in miles/hour   (fsv)
Stretching ratio, in percent     (stretch)
