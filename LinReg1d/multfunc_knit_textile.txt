Dataset:  multfunc_knit_textile.csv

Source: E. Carpus, C. Mihai, A. Dorogan, I. Birliba (2017). 
"Correlation Study of Multifunctional Knitted Textile Structures
Characteristics," Industria Textila, Vol. 68, #2, pp. 131-137.

Description: Relationships among 8 variables for 15 knitted fabric samples.
Models were fit relating Thickness and mass to air permeability and
water vapor permeabilitty. Correlations among variables also obtained.

Variable Names:
ID 
dimChngLen    (Dimensional changes in length)
dimChngWid    (     "         "    "  width)
mass
numWales      (number of wales)
numRows       (number of rows)
thickness
waterVprPerm  (water vapor permeability)
airPerm       (air permeability)
