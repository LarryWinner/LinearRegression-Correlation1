Dataset:  breathtests.csv

Source: R. Poon, B. T. Hodgson, I. Hinberg, C. Rowatt (1987). "Evaluation of
Three Pocket-Size Breath Alcohol Analyzers," Canadian Society of Forensic Science Journal,
Vol. 20, #1, pp. 19-27

Description: 
60 breath alcohol analyzer readings(g/dL) based on the Breathalyzer machine (gold standard)
and Intoxilizer (handheld) and Alco-Sensor (handheld). Authors fit linear regression relating
each handheld device as (Y) and Breathalyzer as X. Also computed sensitivities and 
specificities of the devices relative to gold standard with 0.08 as the negative/positive cut-off.
Data approximated from Figures 7 and 8.

Variable names
breath
intox
alcoSens
