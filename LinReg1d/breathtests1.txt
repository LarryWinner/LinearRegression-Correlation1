Dataset:  breathtests1.csv

Source: M.D. Taylor and B.T. Hodgson (1995). "Blood/Breath Correlations: Intoxilizer 5000C,
Alcotest 7110, and Breathalyzer 900A Breath Alcohol Analyzers," Canadian Society of Forensic 
Science Journal, Vol. 28, #2, pp. 153-164.

Description:
Alcohol measurements (units = mg%) for 15 subjects, each measured twice by 4 devices:
1=Blood, 2=Breathalyzer 900A, 3=Intoxilizer 5000C, 4=Alcotest 7110.
Subjects are labelled 1-3, 7-18 (4-6 had no blood measurements)

Variable Names:
subjectID
readNum       (1 or 2)
device    (levels given above)
mgPct     (alcohol level)
