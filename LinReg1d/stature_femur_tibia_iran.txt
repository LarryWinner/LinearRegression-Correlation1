Dataset: stature_femur_tibia_iran.csv

Source: M. Razemi, V. Shokri, A. Ghanbari, Z. Salehi, K. A. Niknami (2019).
"Stature Estimation in Iranian Population from X-ray Measurement of Femur and Tibia Bones,"
Journal of Forensic Radiology and Imaging, Vol. 19, 
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jofri.2019.100343

Description:
Heights, femur, and tibia lengths for 166 Iranian adults (74 females and 92 males)
between ages 20-40. Data approximated from figures in paper.

Variable Names:
gender  (1=F, 2=M)
tibia (in centimeters)
