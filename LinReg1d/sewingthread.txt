Dataset:  sewingthread.csv

Source: V.K. Midha, S. Sharma, V. Gupta (2016). "Prediction of Sewing
Thread Consumption for Lockstitch Using Regression Model," Research
Journal of Textile and Apparel, Vol. 20, #3, pp. 155-163

Description: Results of an experiment relating sewing thread consumption 
to thickness, stitch density, elongation,
and fiber type (Cotton/Polyester)


Variable Names:
serNum    (run number)
thick
stitchDen
elong
cotton = 1 if Cotton, 0 if polyester
sewThrdCns
