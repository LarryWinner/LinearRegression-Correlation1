Dataset:  fighterjetcosts.csv

Source: A. Bongers and J.L. Torres (2014) "Technological Change in U.S. Jet Aircraft."
Research Policy, Vol. 43, pp. 1570-1581.

Data regarding (log) costs of n = 62 military fighter jets to the following predictors:
Trend (Year - 1943)
Thrust (log)
Climb (log)
Maximum Takeoff Weight (log)
Basic Avionics (0/1)
Advanced Avionics (0/1)
Stealth (0/1)

Variable names
Aircraft
FlyawayCost
FFD (First Flight Date)
Year1 (FFD-1943)
Thrust
Climb
MTOW
basAvionics
advAvionics 
Stealth
