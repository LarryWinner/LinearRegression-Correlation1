Dataset: newzealand_wool.csv

Source: S. Tandon (2015). "Inter-Fibre Cohesion Behaviour of New Zealand
Wools," Research Journal of Textile and Apparel, Vol. 19, #3, pp. 16-22.

Description: Experiment relating sliver strength of wools (tenacity and
specific energy-t-break) to: core bulk, barbe, mean fibre diameter,
and medullation for 21 wool types.

Variable Names:
woolID
woolType
coreBulk   (cm^3/g)
barbe      (mm)
fbrDiam    (mu m)
medulla    (%)
gst        (sliver tenacity, in gf/ktex)
gse        (sliver specific energy-to-break, in gf.cm/ktex)
