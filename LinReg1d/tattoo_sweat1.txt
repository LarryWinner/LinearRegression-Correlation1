Dataset: tattoo_sweat1.csv

Source: M.J. Luetkemeier, J.M. Hanisko, K.M. Aho (2017). "Skin Tattoos Alter Sweat Rate and NA+ Concentration,"
Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise. DOI: 10.1249/MSS.0000000000001244, pp. 1432-1436.

Description:
Sweat Rates and NA+ Concentrations for n=10 subjects at locations with and without tattoos within subjects.
Could be used for paired t-test, regression/correlation, or multivariate analysis.
Scores approximated from Figures 1 and 2.

Variable Names: 
subject
height  (cm)
weight  (kg)
age  (years)
age_tat   (years)
swt_tat   (sweat rate in mg/cm^2/min)
swt_notat      ""
na_tat    (sweat NA+ in mMol/L
