Dataset: Galton_Data.csv

Source: James A. Hanley (2004). "'Transmuting' Women into Men: Galton's 
  Family Data on Human Stature," The American Statistician, Vol.58, #3, pp.237-243.

Description: "Raw" Data from Francis Galton's notebooks of Adult child heights and mid-parents' heights

Variable Names:
Family
Father   (Height in inches)
Mother   (Height in inches)
Gender   (M/F)
Height   (Height in inches)
Kids     (number of kids in family)
Midparent   (0.5*(Father+1.08*Mother))
AdltChld    (if Gender=M,Height;  if Gender=F,1.08*Height)
