Dataset:  fragrance.csv

Source: H.R. Moskowitz (1986). "Sensory Segmentation of Fragrance Preferences."
Journal of the Society of Cosmetic Chemists, Volume 37, pp.233-247, July/August.

Description: Rating scores for 15 varieties of fragrances (3 brands, 12 test products).
Ratings are averages for 160 women who often wear fragrances on a 0-100 scale.
Overall liking and 6 attributes (see below)
Relation between Overall liking (Y) and Attribute scores (X's) tend to be inverted U-shaped.

Variable names
fragrance  (label)
overall
strength  (of fragrance)
sweet
spicy
flowery
heavy
harsh
