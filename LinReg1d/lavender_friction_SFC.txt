Dataset:  lavender_friction_SFC.csv

Source: S.M. Shafei, A. Nourmohamadid-Moghadami, S. Kamgar (2017).
"Experimental Analysis and Modeling of Frictional Behavior of Lavender
Flowers (Lavandula stoechas L.)," Journal of Applied Research on
Medicinal and Aromatic Plants, Vol. 4, pp. 5-11.

Description: Static friction coefficients (SFC) for lavender flowers
across 5 surfaces (1=glass, 2=aluminum, 3=plywood, 4=galvanized steel,
5=rubber) and 5 moisture contents (4.9, 10.2, 14.8, 18.7, 22.4 d.b.%).

Data generated to match cell means and SDs, n=5 reps/treatment
Authors fit 2-Way ANOVA and simple regression models (w/in surface)

Variable Names:
surface
moisture
SFC
