Dataset:  scottish_recycle.dat

Source: J. Baird, R. Curry, and T. Reid (2013). "Development and Application
of a Multiple Linear Regression Model to Consider the Impact of Weekly
Waste Container Capacity on the Yield from Kerbside Recycling Programs
in Scotland," Waste Management & Research, Vol. 31, pp. 306-314

Description: Recycling capacity, residual capacity, number of extended
materials collected, and yield of extended materials collected for
31 Scottish local authorities

Variables/Columns
Local Authority    1-25
Recycling Capacity  (liters/week)  27-33
Residual Capacity       "     "    35-41
Number of extended materials collected  49
Yield of extended materials  (kg/hh/wk)  51-57
