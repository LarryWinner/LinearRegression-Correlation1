Dataset:  paddyfields_slr.dat

Source: J. Chanying, P. Junzheng (1998). "Relationship Between Bearing 
Capacity and Depth of Soft Layer of Paddy Fields in South China," 
Journal of Terramechanics, Vol. 35, pp. 225-228

Description: Bearing Capacity at 10cm depth measured by a penetrometer (Y)
and depth of soft layer (X) in 51 samples of paddy fields.

Vatiables/Columns
Depth of soft layer (cm)   4-8
Bearing Capacity  (kPa)    10-16
