Dataset:  paddyfields_ttest.dat

Source: J-Z. Pan, Z-X. Lu(1998). "Relationship Between Paddy Soil Adhesion
to Steel and to Rubber," Journal of Terramechanics, Vol. 35, pp. 155-158.

Description: 43 pairs of samples of adhesion of paddy soil to steel
and rubber. Analyzed as independent samples, but seems it should be paired.

Variables/Columns
Sample number   7-8
Place (site) id   15-16
Adhesion to steel (kPa)  20-24
Adhesion to Rubber (kPa)  28-32
