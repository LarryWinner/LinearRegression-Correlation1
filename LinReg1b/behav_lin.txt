Dataset: behav_lin.dat

Source: M. Pinhas, J. Tzelgov, D. Ganor Stern (2011). "Estimating Linear
Effects in ANOVA Designs: The Easy Way," Behavior Research Methods, online
first, DOI 10.3758/s13248-011-0172-y

Description: Response Times as a function of intrapair distance
between numbers when subjects are asked to state which is larger.
Can be fit as repeated measures ANOVA with linear trend or
Random Coefficient Regression model.

Variables/Columns
Subject    8
Intrapair distance   16
Response Time    18-24
