Dataset:  dyad_hlm.dat

Source: P. Gadreau, M-C. Fecteau, and S. Perreault (2010). 
"Multi-Level Modeling of Dyadic Data in Sport Sciences: Conceptual,
Statistical, and Practical Issues," Measurement in Physical Education
and Exercise Science, Vol.14,pp29-50.

Description: Data on perfomance, relationship quality, motivation, and
cohesion among 33 sports dyads (badminton, figure skating, synchronised
swimming). Various multi-level models fit (i=dyad, j=individual w/in dyad):

Null model:
Level 1:   Y_ij = B_0i + e_ij
Level 2:   B_0i = G_00 + u_0i

IAO Model:
Level 1:   Y_ij = B_0i + B_1i(Indiv Motivation_ij) + e_ij
Level 2:   B_0i = G_00 + G_01(Dyad Cohesion_i) + G_02(Dyad Motivation_i) +u_0i
           B_1i = G_10

SAO Model:
Level 1:   Y_ij = B_0i + B_1i(Indiv Motivation_ij) + e_ij
Level 2:   B_0i = G_00 + G_01(Dyad Cohesion_i) + G_02(Dyad Motivation_i) + u_0i
           B_1i = G_10 + G_11(Dyad Cohesion_i) + G_12(Dyad Motivation_i) + u_1i


Variables/Columns
Dyad ID    7-8
Individual ID (within Dyad)  16
Individual ID (Overall)    23-24 
Individual's Perceprtion of dyad performance  27-32
Individual's perception of relationship quality  34-40
Individual's self-determined motivation  42-48
Partner's self-determined motivation   50-56
Individual's perception of dyadic cohesion  58-64
Partner's perception of dyadic cohesion  66-72
Dyad's Cohesion score (mean of individuals')   74-80
Dyad's Motivation score ( "  "   ")     82-88
Dyad's Centered Cohesion score       90-96
Dyad's Centered Motivation score    98-104
Dyad's Centered Motivation Score + 1 SD   106-112
Dyad's Centered Motivation Score - 1 SD   114-120
Dyad's Centered Cohesion Score + 1 SD   122-128
Dyad's Centered Cohesion Score - 1 SD   130-136
