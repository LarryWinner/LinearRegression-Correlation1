Dataset:   alc_elim.dat

Source: M. Pavlic, P. Grubweiser, K. Libisiller, Walter Rabl (2007).
"Elimination Rates of Breath Alcohol," Forensic Science International,
Vol. 171, pp. 16-21

Description: Breath alcohol elimination rates (mg per litre per hour)
and Blood alcohol elimination rates (g per litre per hour) measured
on a sample of 59 Austrians (32 M, 27 F). Used to be able to back calculate
pre-measured levels. Legal limits in Austria and many European countries
are 0.5g/L BAC and 0.25mg/L BrAC.

Variables/Columns
Subject ID   7-8
Gender   16   /* 1=Male, 2=Female  */
BrAC (mg/L/h)   18-24
BAC  (g/L/h)    26-32
