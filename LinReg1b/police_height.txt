Dataset:  police_height.dat

Source: S.Q. Lafi and J.B. Kaneene (1992). "An Explanation of the Use
of Principal-Components Analysis to Detect and Correct for Multicollinearity,"
Preventive Veterinary Mediciine, Vol. 13, Issue 4, pp. 261-275.

Description: Standing Height (Y) and multiple correlated predictors
for a sample of 33 female police-department applicants.
Predictors: Sitting height, upper arm length, forearm length,
hand length, upper leg length, lower leg length, foot length,
100*forearm/upper arm, 100*lower leg/upper leg
Typos in published table have been corrected.

Variables/Columns
Applicant ID   7-8
standing height (cm)  10-16
sitting height (cm) 18-24
upper arm (cm)  26-32
forearm (cm)   34-40
hand (cm)  42-48
upper leg (cm)  50-56
lower leg (cm)  58-64
foot  (inches?)  66-72
100(forearm/upper arm)   74-80 
100(lower leg/upper leg)  82-88
