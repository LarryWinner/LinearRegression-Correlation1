Dataset:  std_intensity.dat

Source: T.R.M. De Beer, G.J. Vergote, W.R.G. Baeyens, J.P. Remon, C. Vervaet
and F. Verpoort (2004). "Development and Validation of a Direct,
non-Destructive Quantitative Method for MedroxyprogesteroneAcetate in a 
Pharmaceutical Suspension Using FT-Raman Spectroscopy," European Journal
of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Vol. 23, pp. 355-362

Description: Simple Linear Regression relating measured peak intensities (Y)
to standard suspension Concentration. 4 concentrations, 6 reps/conc.

Variables/Columns
Standard Suspension Concentration   (mg/ml)  1-8
Measured Peak Intensities  (AU)   10-16
