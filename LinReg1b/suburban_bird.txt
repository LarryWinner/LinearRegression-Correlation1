Dataset: suburban_bird.dat

Source: T.R. Vale and G.R. Vale (1976). "Suburban Bird Populations in
West-Central California," Journal of Biogeography, Vol. 3, pp. 157-165

Description: Number of birds observed in 17 West-Central CA suburbs,
age of suburb and habitat index

Variables/Columns
Suburb    1-20
Year built   25-28
Years before 1975   35-36
Habitat index  40-44   /* Function of house, utilities, and shrubs/trees */
Number of individual birds observed  50-52
