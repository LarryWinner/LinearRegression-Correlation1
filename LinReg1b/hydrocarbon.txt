Dataset: hydrocarbon.dat

Source: A.K.A.A. Razzak, E.T. Hashim, and H.N. Al-Shurchi (2006). "New 
Statistical Concepts for Prediction Viscosity, Critical Temperature,
and Critical Pressure of Homologous Hydrocarbon Series (C_nH_(2n+2),"
Petroleum Science and Technology, Vol.24, pp1431-1435.


Description: Data used to fit nonlinear regressions for 3 Dependent
Variables (viscosity, critical pressure, critical temperature) as 
functions of carbon number (C).
Model:  Y = A1*(C^A2)*(2+2C)^A3    where A1,A2,A3 are parameters to
be estimated.

Variables/Columns
Carbon Number 7-8
Viscosity 10-16
Critical Pressure 18-24
Critical Temperature  26-32
