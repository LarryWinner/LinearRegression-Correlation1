Dataset:  leather_jacket.dat

Source: C-C. Matenciuc, S. Mitu, E. Pintilie, and N. Ciuhat (2009).
"The Analysis in 2D and 3D Systems of the Relationship Betweem the Main
Comfort Parameters of the Clothing Structures with Coat or Jacket Made
of Natural Leather - Part II," Annals of the Oradea University Fasicle of
Textile Leatherwork. Vol. X, pp. 329-334


Description: Relation between comfort characteristics and ensemble 
characteristics. Primary responses: Total heat transfer coefficient and
Resistance to vapor passage. Primary predictors: Air permeability of
the ensemble and thickness of ensemble. Models fit in linear and quadratic
forms.

Variables/Columns
Item ID #   7-8
Air permeability of ensemble (m^3/(min*m^2))   10-16
Thickness of ensemble (mm)   18-24
Resistance to air passage (m^2*h*mm/kg)  26-32
Resistance to vapor passage (m^2*h*mm/g)   34-40
Total heat transfer coefficient (kcal/(m^2*h*degC))   42-48
Total Thermal Resistance (m^2*h*degC/kcal)   50-56
Capacitive Thermal Insulation Index    58-64
Required Heat Flux (kcal/(m^2*h))   66-72
