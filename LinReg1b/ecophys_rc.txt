Dataset:  ecophys_rc.dat

Source: C. Potvin, M.J. Lechowicz, S. Tardif (1990). "The Statistical Analysis
of Ecophysiological Response Curves Obtained from Experiments Involving
Repeated Measures," Ecology 71(4), pp.1389-1400.

Description: Nonlinear Growth Curvers for 3 plants each from 2
locations and 2 Treatments at 7 levels of CO2 concentration.
Model fit:   y = A(1-exp(-k*(dose-x0)))

Variables/Columns
Location    8   /* 1=Quebec,  2=Mississippi  */
Treatment   16  /* 1=Control,   2=Chilled  */
Plant ID    23-24
CO2 Concentration   28-32
C02 Uptake Rate    35-40
