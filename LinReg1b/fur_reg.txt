Dataset: fur_reg.dat

Source: H.W. Pyszczyk (1989). "Consumption and Ethnicity: An Example from the 
Fur Trade in Western Canada," Journal of Anthropological Archaeology,
Vol. 8, pp. 213-249.

Description: Artifact/luxury goods diversities as function of
quantity, occupation length, ethnicity, and region for
8 forts in Western Canada.

Variables/Columns
Total artifact diversity (# of types)  5-8
Luxury goods diversity  (# of types)  13-16
quantity of artifacts   20-24
Occupation Length (years)  23-24
Ethnicity   32  /* 1=Orkney,  0=French Canadian  */
Region   40  /* 1=Sakatchewan, 0=Athabaska  */
