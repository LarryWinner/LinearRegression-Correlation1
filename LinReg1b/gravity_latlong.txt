Dataset: gravity_latlong.dat

Source: T.C. Mendenhall (1891). "Determinations of Gravity with the New
Half-Second Pendulums of the Coast and Geodetic Survey at Stations on the
Pacific Coast, in Alaska, and the Base Stations, Washington, D.C., and
Hoboken, N.J.," Report of the Superintendent of the U.S. Coast and
Geodetic Survey,
Appendix No. 15-1891, pp. 503-564

Description: Gravity (in Dynes) at station and reduced to sea level, based on 
observations made by 3 half-second pendulums made at 13 stations
in the U.S. and British Columbia in 1891.

Variables/Columns:
Station    1-20
Latitude   21-28
Longitude   29-36
Sea Level   37-44
Gravity (at station)  45-54
Gravity (reduced to sea level)  55-64
