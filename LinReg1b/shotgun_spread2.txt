Dataset:  shotgun_spread2.csv

Source: I. Cakir, G. Cetin, H.B. Uner, E. Albek (2003)> "Shot Range Estimation Based
on Pellet Distribution in Shots with a Pump-Action Shotgun," Forensic Science International,
Vol. 132, pp. 211-215

Description: Results of experiment measuring maximal vertical and horizontal
spreads (mm) of pellets shot from a Hugsan (12/76 Magnum, 52 cm cylinder bored barrel)
and cartridges (70mm) by MKE.
Pellet Diameters (mm): 2, 3.5, 5, 8
Shot Ranges (m): 0.7,0.8,0.9,1,2,3,5,10,15,20,25
Regression models fit relating Vertical and Horizontal distances to Shot Range by pellet size
5 Replicates per pellet size and shot range

Variable Names:
pellDiam
shotRng
shotID    (not used in analysis)
vertDist
horzdist
