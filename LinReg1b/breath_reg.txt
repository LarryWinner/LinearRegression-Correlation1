Dataset:  breath_reg.dat

Source: R.G. Gullberg (1995). "Repeatability of Replicate Breath Alcohol
Measurements Collected in Short Time Intervals," Science & Justice,
Vol. 35, #1, pp 5-9


Description: Results of 10 measurements each of blood alcohol on each
of 9 subjects. Author fit simple linear regression on time for each subject.
Data simulated to match results.

Variables/Columns
Subject   8
male dummy    16
Time       23-24
Blood alcohol concentration    26-32
Subject mean bac    34-40
subject centered bac   42-48
