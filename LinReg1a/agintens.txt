Dataset: agintens.dat

Source: B.L. Turner, II; R.Q. Hanham, A.V. Portararo (1977). "Population
Pressure and Agricultural Intensity," Annals of the Association of
American Geographers, Vol 67, #3, pp. 384-396.

Description: Factors relating to Agricultural Intensity in 29 regions.
Factors include: population density, whether livestock are present,
mean precipitation, length of dry season, alluvial/hydromporhic
indicator, cereals indicator.


Variables/Columns
Location id   7-8
agricultural intensity   10-16
log(ag intensity) 18-24
population density  27-32
livestock indicator   40
mean precipitation   42-48
alluvial/hydromorphic indicator  56
cereal crop indicator  64
