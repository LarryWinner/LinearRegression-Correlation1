Dataset:  atltime1.dat

Source: Bureau of Transportation Statistics (www.bts.gov)

Description: Airborne times of all domestic Delta Air Lines flights
departing from Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport
(Atlanta) in October, 2004.


Variables/Columns

Day of month 1-8   
Flight Number  9-16
Tail Number   17-24   /* Plane identifier  */
Destination airport  25-32
Airborne time (minutes)  33-40
Distance  (miles)  41-48   /* Great circle distance betwen airports */
City ID    49-56
