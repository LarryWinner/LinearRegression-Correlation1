Dataset: atlschool.dat

Source: E.R. Enlow (1938). "Do Small Schools Mean Large Costs?," Peabody
Journal of Educaltion, Vol. 16, #1, pp. 1-11

Description: Per-Pupil Costs, Average Daily Attendance, Average Monthly
Teacher salary, Percent Attendance, and Pupil-Teacher ratio for the 44 White
Elementary schools in Atlanta

Variables/Columns:
School  1-15
Per-Pupil Cost  (Dollars)   17-23
Average daily Attendance   25-31
Average Monthly Teacher Salary (Dollars)  33-39
Percent Attendance       41-47
Pupil/Teacher ratio    50-55
