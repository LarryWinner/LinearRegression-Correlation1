Dataset: heatwave.dat

Source:  P. Pirard et al (2005). "Summary of the Mortality Impact Assessment
of the 2003 Heat Wave in France", Eurosurveillance Monthly Releases,
Volume 10, Issue 7-8

Description: Mortality data for 13 cities in France for August1-19 
and comparison figures for 1999-2002, as well as temperature data
over the period.

Variables/Columns
City    1-13
2003 Deaths   19-22
Mean deaths 1999-2002    26-30
Excess deaths in 2003 (%)  36-38
Days w/ high temp >35C    45-46
Days w/ low temp >22C    53-54
Change in mean temp over period (C)  60-62
