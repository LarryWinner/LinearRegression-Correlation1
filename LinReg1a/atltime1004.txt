Dataset: atltime1004.dat

Souce:  U.S. Bureau of Transportation Statistics

Description: Flight times (scheduled and actual) and taxi-out times
of all Delta outbound flights from Atlanta in October 2004. Note that
cancelled flights will vave actual flight time=0.

Variables/Columns
Flight Date  1-2
Flight Number  6-9
Tail Number   13-18
Destination city  22-24
Taxi-out time (minutes)  28-31
Scheduled flight time  35-38
Actual flight time   42-45
distance (miles)   49-52
