Dataset:  rtw.dat

Source: D. Gilbert (1966). "A Statistical Analysis of
the Right to Work Conflict", Industrial and Labor Relations Review",
Vol 19, #4, pp 533-537.

Description: State level data on industrial conflict, as well as
percent union workers, percent working in relatively non-unionized
industries, percent working in agriculture, and an indicator of whether
the state has is a union-shop state.

Variables/Columns:
State Postal Abbreviation   1-2
Degree of conflict (1957-1962)  13-16  /* % of worker hours lost in stoppages */
Degree of Unionization  21-24   /* % of workforce */
Union-shop dummy   32  /* 1=Non-Right to work, 0=Right to work  */
Sector A Workers  37-40 /* % in food, textiles and apparel  */
Ag force  45-48   /* Percent working in agriculture  */
