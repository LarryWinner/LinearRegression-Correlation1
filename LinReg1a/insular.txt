Dataset: insular.dat

Source: F. Vuilleumier (1970). "Insular Biogeography in Continental Regions.
I. The Northern Andes of South America," American Naturalist. Vol.104:373-388.

Description: Patterns of bird species among "islands" of mountain
tops in 15 regions of the Andes.
